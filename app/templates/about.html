About NeuroCare AI

An AI-powered simulation platform for testing neurological disease triage under real rural India constraints.

⚠️ Problem Statement

Rural India faces a critical gap in neurological care: poor internet connectivity (<50 kbps), limited specialist access, and delayed diagnosis of time-sensitive diseases like Stroke and Meningitis. NeuroCare AI simulates how AI triage systems perform under these real-world constraints — before deployment.

🤖 ML Architecture

Primary: Logistic Regression

Lightweight, fast inference (<5ms), suitable for low-resource devices. Trained separately for each disease with class-balanced weights. Outputs calibrated probabilities for risk stratification.

Fallback: Rule-Based System

Clinical heuristics activate when the ML model is unavailable (offline/cache miss). Example: IF slurred speech + weakness → Stroke High Risk. Zero latency, zero bandwidth.
